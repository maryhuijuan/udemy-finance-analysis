A concise, 20-lecture course with active student feedback will outperform a 100-lecture course with no reviews.3. Niche Quality vs. Mass PopularityFinding: There is nearly zero correlation (0.08) between a course's popularity and its rating.Strategic Recommendation: Market researchers should look for "hidden gems"—small, high-rated courses with low subscriber counts—as these represent underserved niches with high student satisfaction that are ripe for scaling.
