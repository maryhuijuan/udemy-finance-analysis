Niche Quality vs. Mass PopularityFinding: There is nearly zero correlation (0.08) between a course's popularity and its rating.Strategic Recommendation: Market researchers should look for "hidden gems"—small, high-rated courses with low subscriber counts—as these represent underserved niches with high student satisfaction that are ripe for scaling.

Summary: The "Niche Expert" Market Strategy

1. Seed with Free, Scale with Paid: Use a free "Intro" course to build that initial 0.78 correlation (reviews/subscribers) quickly.

2. Target the Premium Tier: Once you have 50+ reviews, move the course to the Premium price group. Your data shows this group maintains high satisfaction ($3.9$ rating).

3. Optimize for Results, Not Length: Stop adding lectures just to make the course "longer." Focus on the Engagement Rate (reviews/subscribers) by keeping content tight and actionable.
